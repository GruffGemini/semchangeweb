Explore diachronic changes in the usage of words

Welcome to SEMCHANGE — a web service for analyzing diachronic changes in the usage of words.

Visualizations

The current version of our app has following visualizations:

  • Word Changes: comparison of word changes
  • Word Clusters: exploring clusters of word's different meanings

Supported languages: English, Russian

Data

English

To get results for English, we used Clean Corpus of Historical American English (CCOHA).
Time coverage for each corpus:

  • Corpus 1: 1810-1860
  • Corpus 2: 1960-2010

Russian

For Russian we used Diachronic datasets of the RNC.
Time coverage for each corpus:

  • Corpus 1: 1700–1916
  • Corpus 2: 1992–2016
